KaffeeSATS

Bitcoin-Powered Coffee Machine

What is KaffeeSATS?

KaffeeSATS is an open-source Bitcoin coffee machine powered by the Lightning Network. Users pay with Lightning or NFC Bolt Cards and unlock a fresh cup of coffee instantly.

Features

⚡ Lightning Payments

Instant Bitcoin payments via LNbits and ZapBox.

💳 NFC Bolt Cards

Tap and pay with NFC-enabled Lightning cards.

☕ Real Coffee

A modified Bosch Tassimo machine controlled by Lightning payments.

Open Source

All hardware modifications, software, 3D printed parts and documentation are available on GitHub.
