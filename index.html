Two Bitcoin Coffee Machines

⚡ Offline Bitcoin Switch

Uses a Sunton ESP32 smart display and the LNbits Offline Bitcoin Switch. Users pay with Lightning, receive a secret PIN and unlock the machine.

💳 ZapBox Online Edition

Uses ZapBox, LNbits and NFC Bolt Card support. The ZapBox sends a 5V signal to an internal relay after payment.

How It Works

1. Pay

Scan a Lightning Invoice or tap a Bolt Card.

2. Unlock

The relay enables the original Tassimo start button.

3. Press

Press the yellow Tassimo button.

4. Enjoy

The coffee machine starts brewing.

About KaffeeSATS

KaffeeSATS is an open-source Bitcoin hardware project that connects Lightning payments with real-world machines.

The idea is simple: Bitcoin should not only be explained — it should be experienced. With KaffeeSATS, visitors can scan a Lightning invoice, tap a Bolt Card, unlock a coffee machine and receive a real cup of coffee.

The project was built to demonstrate how LNbits, ZapBox, ESP32 hardware, relays and 3D printed parts can work together in a practical Bitcoin use case.

Built by a Bitcoiner, for Bitcoiners.

Why Bitcoin?

Bitcoin enables instant, borderless and permissionless payments.

KaffeeSATS demonstrates how Lightning payments can interact directly with physical hardware in the real world.

No cash. No cards. No intermediaries. Just Bitcoin and coffee.
