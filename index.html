KaffeeSATS

Pay With Lightning. Drink Coffee.

Open-source Bitcoin coffee machines powered by the Lightning Network, LNbits, Offline Bitcoin Switch and ZapBox.

View on GitHub Downloads

Two Bitcoin Coffee Machines

⚡ Offline Bitcoin Switch

Uses a Sunton ESP32 smart display and the LNbits Offline Bitcoin Switch. Users pay with Lightning, receive a secret PIN and unlock the machine.

💳 ZapBox Online Edition

Uses ZapBox, LNbits and NFC Bolt Card support. The ZapBox sends a 5V signal to an internal relay after payment.
