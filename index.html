About The Project

KaffeeSATS is an open-source Bitcoin coffee machine project powered by the Lightning Network. It demonstrates real-world Bitcoin payments through modified Bosch Tassimo coffee machines.

Offline Bitcoin Switch Edition



Powered by the LNbits Offline Bitcoin Switch and a Sunton ESP32 Smart Display. Users pay with Lightning, receive a secret PIN and unlock the coffee machine.

ZapBox Online Edition



Powered by ZapBox, LNbits and NFC Bolt Cards. Lightning payments unlock the machine through an internal relay interface.
